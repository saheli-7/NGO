Food Disposal Management
Waste food management, often referred to as food waste management or food waste reduction, is the process of efficiently handling, minimizing, and properly disposing of food waste to reduce its environmental impact and optimize resource utilization. This practice aims to address the significant issue of food waste, which has social, economic, and environmental implications. Waste food management is critical for several reasons. It helps conserve natural resources, reduces greenhouse gas emissions, saves money for businesses and households, alleviates hunger, and promotes sustainability in the food system. Efforts to manage food waste have gained increasing attention in recent years as a means to address global food security and environmental challenges.
